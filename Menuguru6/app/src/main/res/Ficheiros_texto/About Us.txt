What's Menu Guru?

It is an online platform that allows you to find a variety of restaurants, see restaurant's menus, access to special campaigns and book tables online.


How does Menu Guru works?

Menu Guru allows you to find restaurants around you, check our suggestions and search restaurants according to your preferences.

Menu Guru allows you to book tables online, rescue vouchers directly from our platform in the most varied places paying directly at the restaurant.

Create your list of favourite restaurants and share it with your friends.

See the daily menus, restaurant's menus and special campaigns.

Verify wether the restaurant is open or closed.

Get the directions and go to the restaurant easily.

Find out what's going on, share your opinion and inspire more people to know the restaurants you have visited.

Allow you to live new experiences.
